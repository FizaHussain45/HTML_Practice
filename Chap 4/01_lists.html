Steps to bake a cake

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour your cake pan.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Cream the butter and sugar together.
  5. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well.
  6. Stir in the dry ingredients and milk alternately.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  8. Bake for 25–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  10. Remove from the pan and allow it to cool completely before frosting.
